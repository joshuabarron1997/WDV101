About Us


Founded in 1996 we are Springfield's only no-kill animal shelter.
Since its opening Pets to Go has placed an estimated 18,000 homeless cats and dogs in new households. Where do these animals come from? 40% are owner surrenders
and the other 60% are animals we rescues from shelters that euthanize.

We have a dedicated staff of 15 (half are part-time).
And our 200-plus volunteers are our backbone (thank you volunteers and donors!).
Many of the rescued animals who come to Pets to Go
get more love and attention in our shelter than they've ever known before.
Our shelter continues to grow and improve. Drop by and get to know us better!
